Ever since the EKpack started in 2016 the goal has been simple: Provide the best experience customized for EleKTriiK's tastes, while preserving a rather vanilla look and feel to be compatible with all minecraft constructions, minigames and servers. Using the famous Faithful by Vattic as a base, the pack evolved over the years to become an add-on to the newest faithful version (classic or not).

THe EKpack is driven by what inspire the author, may it be other ressource pack, as those credited below, or other games such as minecraft story mode. THe ressource pack use a mix of various online ressources and custom creation from its author.
